About Us

Peer mentoring is the mechanism to connect the institution and mentee. Nonetheless, many mentoring programs focus training on organizational needs (procedural, policies, protocols), sociopsychology support (relational) and the transactional (asset building). Strive2Gether’s mentor training is a dialectical process in which the mentors start to think critically and analytically about their own experience(s) of processing prior interpretations of “meaning” to create a new a revised sense of individual meaning within the context of their personal ecosystem.

Mission

The mission of Strive2Gether is to facilitate mentor development through engagement, knowledge building and psychosocial support needed for transformation in their educational praxis. Strive2Gether’s aim is to educate and understand theory and connecting theory to practice in the transformation of mentee populations.

Vision

The vision of Strive2Gether is to partner with mentors to empower them with the necessary skillsets to be successful in supporting mentees in creating their own individual, dynamic life plans and support them in formulating the goals and necessary positive habitudes to achieve them.
