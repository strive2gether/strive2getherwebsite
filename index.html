About Us

Mission

The mission of EOP Mentoring is to facilitate first year student develpment of EOP Scholars Academy participants by applying upper classmanengagement and psychosocial support with the guidance of the EOP Advising Office.

Goals

1
Student Retention: Decrease sophmore attrition by 2% in four years.
2
Student Persistence: An individual-level variable.
3
Student support: Mentoring and academic advising contacts.

Vision

EOP Mentors vision is to partner with first year students to empower them with creating their own indivual, dynamic life plans and support them in formulating the goals and necessary positive habitudes to achieve them.
